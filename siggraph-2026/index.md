# SIGGRAPH 2026

Assumptions: attend the conference in Los Angeles, fly through LAX, stay at
The Ritz-Carlton Los Angeles, and walk between the hotel and conference rather
than rent a car.

## At a Glance

- **Conference:** [SIGGRAPH 2026](https://s2026.siggraph.org/)
- **Dates:** Sunday, July 19 through Thursday, July 23, 2026.
- **Exhibition:** Tuesday, July 21 through Thursday, July 23.
- **Location:** [Los Angeles Convention Center](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Los_Angeles_Convention_Center),
  1201 S Figueroa Street, Los Angeles, CA 90015
  ([map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Los%20Angeles%20Convention%20Center%2C%201201%20S%20Figueroa%20St%2C%20Los%20Angeles%2C%20CA%2090015)).

As of July 13, 2026, SIGGRAPH lists the conference for July 19-23 at the Los
Angeles Convention Center. The exhibition runs July 21-23, from 10:00 a.m. to
5:00 p.m. Tuesday and Wednesday and 10:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m. Thursday.
[[1]](#source-1)[[2]](#source-2)

Use the [full conference schedule](https://s2026.conference-schedule.org/) to
build a personal agenda. SIGGRAPH says the schedule is mobile-friendly and
serves as the on-site guide; there is no separate conference app.
[[2]](#source-2)

## Hotel

**[The Ritz-Carlton, Los Angeles](https://www.ritzcarlton.com/en/hotels/laxlz-the-ritz-carlton-los-angeles/overview/)**
900 W Olympic Boulevard, Los Angeles, CA 90015
Telephone: +1 213-743-8800
[Map](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=The%20Ritz-Carlton%20Los%20Angeles%2C%20900%20W%20Olympic%20Blvd%2C%20Los%20Angeles%2C%20CA%2090015)

The hotel is in L.A. LIVE, beside Crypto.com Arena and just north of the
convention center. The official hotel page lists check-in at 4:00 p.m.,
check-out at 11:00 a.m., no airport shuttle, and LAX 17.2 miles from the
property. [[3]](#source-3)

## Daily Commute

**Plan to walk.** The hotel and convention center are roughly a half mile apart;
allow about 10-15 minutes from the hotel lobby to the convention-center
campus, plus extra time to find the correct hall or meeting room.

[Walking route: Ritz-Carlton to Los Angeles Convention Center](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=The%20Ritz-Carlton%20Los%20Angeles%2C%20900%20W%20Olympic%20Blvd%2C%20Los%20Angeles%2C%20CA%2090015&destination=Los%20Angeles%20Convention%20Center%2C%201201%20S%20Figueroa%20St%2C%20Los%20Angeles%2C%20CA%2090015&travelmode=walking)

- Leave 20-25 minutes before the first session on the first morning. LACC is a
  large campus, and the useful entrance depends on the session room.
- Do not plan to drive between the hotel and conference. The walk is shorter
  than the parking process, and the hotel currently lists valet parking at
  $71.50 per day. [[3]](#source-3)
- If walking is uncomfortable, use a short taxi or ride-app trip, but expect
  construction-related pickup delays.
- Pico Metro station serves the A and E lines and is about an eight-minute walk
  from LACC, but Metro is unnecessary for the hotel-to-conference commute.
  [[4]](#source-4)

### Construction Caveat

As of July 13, 2026, the convention center reports that Pico Boulevard between
Figueroa Street and L.A. Live Way is closed continuously through March 31,
2028. It also lists weekday work on L.A. Live Way through August 1, 2026.
Follow posted pedestrian detours and recheck the LACC transportation page just
before departure. [[4]](#source-4)

Rick Steves-style choices:

- use Smart & Final or Ralphs for a quick supply run;
- walk to Whole Foods only if prepared food or its broader specialty selection
matters.
- Treat the Little Tokyo markets as specialty-food destinations rather
than routine supply runs.
- Cheap-wine choice: try Grocery Outlet first. The chain specifically
promotes its wine selection for bargain hunters, but inventory changes
frequently, so Smart & Final is the closer fallback when predictable selection
matters more than finding the lowest price. [[24]](#source-24)

## Airport: LAX

**Best practical choice:** take a taxi or ride app between LAX and the hotel.
The hotel has no shuttle, and downtown traffic makes the trip time variable.
For the arrival trip, LAX directs standard Uber, Lyft, Opoli, and taxi
passengers to the LAX-it pickup area beside Terminal 1; walk there or take the
free shuttle from the lower arrivals level. Ride apps and taxis can drop
departing passengers directly at their terminal. [[3]](#source-3)[[5]](#source-5)

[Driving route: LAX to The Ritz-Carlton](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Los%20Angeles%20International%20Airport%20%28LAX%29&destination=The%20Ritz-Carlton%20Los%20Angeles%2C%20900%20W%20Olympic%20Blvd%2C%20Los%20Angeles%2C%20CA%2090015&travelmode=driving)

Public-transit fallback: take the LAX FlyAway bus to Union Station, then the
Metro A Line to Pico station and walk to the hotel. This avoids a car but adds a
transfer and is less attractive with luggage. Recheck both services shortly
before travel because schedules and airport construction can change.
[[6]](#source-6)[[4]](#source-4)

## Practical Notes

- Download or print the conference registration confirmation before flying.
- Build the personal schedule before arrival, then leave gaps for the
  exhibition and informal meetings.
- Carry water and a light layer; July is hot outdoors, while conference rooms
  can be cold.
- Keep the arrival day light. LAX-to-downtown travel is too variable to anchor
  a must-attend session immediately after landing.
- Recheck the conference schedule, LACC construction notice, and LAX pickup
  instructions during the week before departure.
